## 2026

### Lejon
Built it for myself, to chop Suno output the way Serato Sample chops samples. Real-beat detection (not just BPM division), AI stem separation, filters, drag-to-DAW export. Somewhere along the way I swapped the square pads for piano keys, noticed the black keys had nothing to do, and ended up with a performance sampler — piano-roll loop sequencer, multi-selection, chopping, scratch effects. [dread.se](https://dread.se)
`active · solo · side-project`

### Loopy
A four-track audio looper for macOS. Plays the first thing you record back at the right BPM and locks the rest to it. Count-in metronome, latency-compensated capture, waveforms drawn on the GPU. JUCE 8, C++20, Metal, lock-free SPSC between UI and audio threads. AI style transfer is in — record a vocal loop, get a guitar, a flute, whatever — with audio pre-processing before conversion so the model has something clean to work with.
`active · solo · side-project`

### ok.proof
Hire the people who get things done with AI. Send a candidate a real task in a sandbox with their pick of frontier model as the assistant. Watch every prompt, decision and iteration in a timeline replay. Rate them on Code Quality, Problem Solving, Communication and Prompt Skill, by hand or by LLM. Templates clone any website or take uploaded files. Built end to end. [okproof.co](https://okproof.co)
`active · solo · side-project`

### Anti Frontend Design
A Claude Code skill. Purple gradients. Inter. Three-column feature grid. Bouncing *Get Started* button. Sarah P., Product Manager at TechCorp. Footer that says *All right reserved.* I packaged it all up so you can summon it on purpose. [github.com/Gori/anti-frontend-design](https://github.com/Gori/anti-frontend-design)
`active · solo · side-project`

### Fjodor Defense
Tower Defense on a real 3D map of Södermalm. Made it with my son Fjodor.
`active · w/ Fjodor · side-project`

### Artistry
A workspace for writing songs and tracking them through production. Lyrics editor on CodeMirror 6 with chord overlay, syllable counts, rhymes and an inline Claude assistant. Songs move across a Kanban from Idea to Done. Multiple audio versions with A/B comparison. Voice memos transcribed by Whisper. Logic Pro project versioning with arrangement-lane diffs. Teleprompter for tracking sessions.
`active · solo · side-project`

## 2016

### Tracklib *(2016–2023)*
A sample marketplace. Partnered with record labels for pre-cleared, pre-priced individual tracks and stems — what used to take an army of lawyers, now a one-click license. Co-built the original product as CPO & Partner. Ran product strategy quarterly to board and investors. Drove the move from per-track purchases to a credit-based subscription: paying users +50% in six months, retention 20% → 60%, revenue/day 10×, users +157%, 5,724 paying subscribers and $52k MRR by October 2020, top-10 earner for many of the labels, $0.8M paid out to rights holders by end of 2020. Helped raise $23.2M total, including a $16.8M Series B led by Sony Innovation Fund. [tracklib.com](https://tracklib.com)
`active · partner · startup`

### We Are Voice
Almost 10% of Sweden sings in a choir every week — that's 600,000+ people, in one of the world's most choir-dense countries. Co-founded an interactive platform for them with Fredrik Berglund, Martin Rolinski and Simon Ljungman. A multitrack player with sopran, alt, tenor, bas and master tracks, sheet music scrolling underneath, plus tools for choirs to rehearse together at distance and perform together in person. Founder & CPO. Directed the product roadmap, prototyped the first versions, ran the external dev team. Raised $1M seed from Bonnier Ventures. [wearevoice.se](https://wearevoice.se/sv)
`active · co-founded · startup`
